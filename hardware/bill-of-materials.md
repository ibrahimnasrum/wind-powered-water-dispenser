# Bill of Materials (BOM)

Based on the project report.

## Electronics
- 8 × DC motor (wind generator)
- Arduino Uno R3
- Infrared (IR) sensor module
- 5V relay module (1-channel)
- Water pump (3V–5V)
- Wires (red/black)

## Mechanical
- Aluminium tin (blades)
- Wood block (base)
- Sticks / supports
- Boxes (housing)
- Toothpaste cap + water bottle cap (couplers)
- Tube / hose

## Tools
- Digital multimeter
- Blower (wind source)

## Recommended add-ons (for stability)
- Bridge rectifier
- DC-DC regulator (stable 5V)
- Energy buffer (battery or supercapacitor)
